Shri Jai Narayan Mahavidyalaya is creating awareness in remote rural area of village of Durgipur. durgipur is one of the biggest revenue village of Tehsil Beruarbari.The literacy percentage in surrounding area comes to be very low. With the motive to extend higher education to the rural people as well as in Backword area Shri Jai Narayan Mahavidyalaya has been established. We have also encourage the students to develope their physical and mental level through various games and sports. We are gladful to sincere support of all concerned, particularly staff members & students of the college for their devotion, dedication and cooperation in all respect. The college is attaining new heights and students to experience he change and participate in achieving excellence.
